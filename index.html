Biography

Dr. Jingzhi Hu is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Intelligent Transmission and Processing Laboratory (ITP Lab), Imperial College London. His current research focuses on wireless communications, signal processing, and the cutting-edge applications of machine learning in communication systems.
